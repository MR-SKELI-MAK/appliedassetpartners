Asset Curation

Curating and showcasing luxury assets such as fine art, historical artifacts, or rare collectibles requires a meticulous and thoughtful approach. Our asset curation services are tailored for high-profile events, museum exhibitions, and exclusive private viewings, ensuring that each piece is handled with the utmost care and presented to enhance its prestige and value.Asset curation is the process of organizing, managing, and assembling a collection of data assets. The goal of asset curation is to make data assets easier to find, provide information about their structure and semantics, and capture any modifications or enhancements made to them. Here are some steps involved in asset curation: Create data assets, Assign metadata, Publish assets to a catalog, and Update metadata. Asset curation can be done manually or automatically.
